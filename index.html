Challenges & The Road Ahead

We recognize the engineering hurdles for a commercial product, which represent exciting areas for future development.

Precision & Calibration

Achieving consistent, certified trip thresholds requires precise manufacturing and calibration to account for ambient temperature and material tolerances.

Arcing at Contact Break

Safely managing the electrical arc created when breaking high fault currents is a critical safety challenge for high-voltage applications.

Long-Term Durability

The Nitinol element must be tested for fatigue over thousands of heating/cooling cycles to ensure long-term reliability.

Commercialization Hurdles

Scaling for mass production involves overcoming material costs, manufacturing complexity, and rigorous safety certifications.
